Put.io Test-drive

April 12 2010

Put.io is an online cloud storage service. few days ago i got the invite from techrunch, only valid for 7 days as a guest user. 据说是个在线云储存服务。前几天拿到了techrunch提供的put.io测试邀请，仅仅是7天的guest user.

Basically, put.io download your files(torrent/rapidshare/rss) for you. you could just watch ur movies online with divx web player(not supported in linux) or listen to mp3s without downloading to ur hard drive. if with a low-speed connection, u could download those files, of course with the help of various download managers.

BTW, put.io is not available for IE!! hoho~ i guess chrome is better supported.

基本上，put.io就是帮你下载文件。如果本地网速快，可以直接在put.io上看电影(divx web player，臃肿，不支持linux)，或者听mp3。如果网速太慢，像我一样教育网，就只能再用迅雷把文件拖下来。上图。

a. enter download link

b.analyze and grab

c.transfer

d.adding subscription. automatically grab media content

e.mp3 playing

f.download files with Xunlei(in spite of how much i hated Xunlei, it’s the educational network users’ savior. when using Xunlei for downloading, u’ll need ur put.io account email and password to connect to put.io’s server)

and that’s pretty much it. put.io offers guest users 50g each for storage and bandwidth. but it’s gonna be a non-free service, which disappointed me a little. in short, it’s just great, especially for collecting big and popular files(i get torrents from piratebay) as well as saving the trouble to manage your podcasts. i might as well enjoy the 3 days left for guest users :)
