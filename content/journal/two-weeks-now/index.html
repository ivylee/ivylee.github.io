Two Weeks Now...

September 16 2006

2 weeks after I became a Senior 2 student, I found myself not got along with my new classmates very well. It seemed everyone just trying to grab their old familiar friends instead of trying to make new acquaintances. As I understand the situation, I think making new friends while we maintain old friendships is necessary. After all, we have to be with new classmates for the next 2 years. How can we not be known to each other?\ About the teachers, I found something very interesting. Facts about teachers are often talked by students, especially facts beside he/she as a teacher. My new math teacher, L, is fond of music and he’s good at composing. I remembered once when I entered the office, he was singing Chinese opera while working which really surprised me. There are many other things about a teacher that we talked about. And through the talking, little things will spread quickly until they are known to some certain number of people. They are not like rumors, but sort of news from the tabloids. Actually no one knows where the information came from or it’s true or not. But curiosity drove us to the joy of revealing some mystery.\ About my former math teacher: on 31st Aug. he was said to be the class teacher of Senior 2 Class 5; on 2nd Sept. he was not in the Senior 2 office; later he was said to be teaching Senior 1 and not much later he was said to be teaching a junior class and Senior 1; the latest news is that he is teaching Senior 1 Class 2&5 and is the classteacher of Senior 1 Class 2.\ Interesting.\
