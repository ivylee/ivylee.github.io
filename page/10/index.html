Live-Android

July.7th 今天发现了 live-android(a LiveCD for Android running on x86 platforms.) ，下载了 iso ，用 Virtualbox mount&boot ，可爱的 android 出现了 … LiveAndroid HOWTO liveandroid v0.2 1. Download liveandroidv0.2.iso.001 / liveandroidv0.2.iso.002 , use HJSplit(http://www.freebyte.com/hjsplit/ or http://www.freebytesoftware.com/download/hjsplit.zip) join it. 我用的是 lxsplit 2. Check md5sum via http://www.pc-tools.net/win32/md5sums/ or http://www.pc-tools.net/files/win32/freeware/md5sums-1.2.zip liveandroidv0.2.iso MD5: 03852bce8cb26aba21d147153c1fb120 skip掉… 3. use liveandroidv0.2.iso in vmware or virtualbox to your taste. of cource, you can burn it as a LiveCD. 4. boot and enjoy!
