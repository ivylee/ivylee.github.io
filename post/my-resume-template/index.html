Minimalistic Resume Template

Here I am. Just arrive, getting used to New York, excited about new life and new things to learn.

LaTeX is very nice for writing structured documents. I used to prepare my resume using moderncv . It was really nice, easy and beautiful. But somehow I find it a little bit too fancy now. I decided to write my own resume class, no coloring no pictures just plain and simple. I used LaTeX for my undergraduate thesis and I modified thesis templates from other sources to fit my thesis format requirements, but I’ve never build a template from ground up myself. So the resume template, it was good exercise. Now I’m not scared of writing LaTeX class files anymore. :)
