after a long hiatus

I want to write again.

But first I needed to migrate my blog from Octopress to Ghost. I was happy with Octopress until I discovered every update/push took forever because I have 200+ posts.

Migration was not easy. Took me almost 4 nights. Essentially the problems for me were:

I didn’t push the markdown files that are supposed to be under source/_posts to github. So I only have a bunch of published html files waiting to be converted back to markdown. During conversion it’s tricky to deal with Chinese characters, took me 1-2 nights to figure out. Loading Octopress markdown files to Ghost database. There’s a nice script available however some tweaks were needed to suit my use case. I decided not to integrate Disqus comments for now. Don’t worry I still have your previous comments in heart.

Please check out this repo if you’re wondering whether my experience might be helpful to you.

Finally I like the combination of Ghost + Buster + Github Pages as described here :) And I’m writing again!
