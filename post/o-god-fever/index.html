O God, Fever!

i guess now my temperature reaches 38 point something, not sure because i don’t have a thermometer…

honestly i don’t remember the last time i wrote something here. it has been exhausting lately and i couldn’t sleep last night which i guess caused today’s fever. bad news is that i might still have to go to classes starting at 8.30 tomorrow….sh!t…

sometimes i look at things i’ve done and blame myself for having done any of them. i could spend those precious time on studying, reading, music, movie…anything i love and don’t have enough time to do. but i did what i’ve done out of enthusiasm too. perplexing thing is i don’t know what should take up more of my time. volunteering is the one thing so far i’ve not discover anything ugly inside. you just go out there and give whatever you can give. it’s a pure joy of giving. but on the other hand, time’s running out. i’ve got homework and lots of study to work on. in this U, you are hardly recognized being a volunteer but you get the scholarship(absolutely not the American version of scholarship) with your grades, your great effort to climb up in the bureacratic system of the League or student union, your participation in tedious competitions and your full attendence of the brain-wash Marxism classes. you must go with the flow or you’ll be drown. i want to be truthful both to myself and to anyone else. i don’t flatter and don’t like to be flattered. there’s nothing i can do to change my surroudings but i won’t compromise. i will no doubt continue to do what i love and when i leave this place, i can say to myself, i’ve utilized my time though it may not seem so in everyone else’s eyes.

i’ve decided to stay in tomorrow.
