I Want Such Laptop

I want a new laptop, mainly because the one I currently have and use is way to heavy for me to carry around.

Besides, I want to taste a bit new techs, like SSD and USB 3.0. I heard SSD significantly saves time, as for USB 3.0, I have a portable hard disk that supports  this new standard unfortunately I haven’t been able to test the transfer speed, which is obviously vitally important to me if I’m using a laptop with 128G SSD.

So I want a laptop that:

  1. is thin and light (including battery stuff);
  2. screen size between 13 in and 14 in;
  3. comes with 128G SSD and USB 3.0;
  4. running Linux on it won’t be much trouble;
  5. long battery life;
  6. good temperature control;
  7. not ugly, not like Apple copycat;
  8. high screen resolution;
  9. nice keyboard;
  10. affordable;

I understand nowadays laptops come with pre-installed OS, I wonder if I don’t want it, is it possible that I’ll get a refund or price cut?

I don’t really care much about anything else. I just want a portable, fast, reliable Linux machine >_< Any advice? TIA.
